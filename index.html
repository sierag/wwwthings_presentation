Two types of sponsors: local vs global

Local Sponsors?

Local sponsors crossing my mind

AGU, Ambrero, HVC, Triple IT, CYSO, Cloud2, BlueDesk, ISSYS, RackTime, Sigma Solutions, Mediamoose, Abovo, Holder, Boozd, Reyez, Uniserver, QBall, RAADHUIS

What's the win for a local sponsor

Hard to connect with (for example) a developer living nearby, working in Amsterdam. A local sponsor can leave an positive impression to the developer, get noticed or even get in straight contact with the developer.

Global Sponsors?

IBM, Layer, MasterCard, Twilio, Vinli, Tesla, WeatherAPI, Yammer, Zalando, LinkedIn, Salesforce, PayPal, LEGO, Leap, Segway, AirBnb, Meerkat

What's the win for a global sponsor

Their target is to let participants work with there API's. Sometimes even it can create new insights or new business cases (long shot).
